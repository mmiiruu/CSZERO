## Design Context

### Users
Thai CS freshmen at Kasetsart University — a mix of eager high-achievers who sought out the club and cautious beginners who were nudged to join. They access the site on mobile during breaks, campus commutes, and late-night study sessions. They're discovering who they are as CS students: curious but uncertain, seeking both technical credibility and a sense of belonging at the same time.

Both Hello World and CS101 target the same freshman audience. Hello World is the broader welcome event (all freshmen); CS101 is the coding-focused track. Both are entry points, not filters.

Two distinct sub-audiences:
- **Prospective/new members:** Landing on the homepage for the first time, judging whether CSKU is worth their time. They need to feel impressed AND welcomed.
- **Registered participants:** Navigating event pages (CS101, Hello World) where immersion and excitement matter more than orientation.

### Brand Personality
**Nerdy · Premium · Technically Sharp**

Not "cool tech startup." Not "friendly university portal." Something more specific: the energy of a CS student who has taste. The interface should feel like it was built by people who read documentation for fun — precise, intentional, with moments of delight that reward attention. Think: the inside of a well-maintained dotfiles repo, or a zine made by a CS club that actually cares. Boring is a failure state.

### Aesthetic Direction

**Dark-first.** New visitors see dark mode by default. The darkness is not aesthetic posturing — it's the natural environment of someone staring at a terminal at 11pm. This applies to the main site shell only. Events are their own contained worlds with forced themes.

**Reference (jwc.in.th):** Admire its full commitment to a visual metaphor carried through every section, its custom decorative elements that feel handcrafted, and its narrative coherence. Apply that same commitment to theme but with CSKU's sharper, more technically-inflected personality. Where JWC is warm pastel scrapbook, CSKU is dark precision instrument.

**Anti-reference:** Startup SaaS — no purple-to-blue gradients, no glowing card grids, no hero metric layouts, no rounded-rect-with-drop-shadow card monoculture, no "modern" geometric sans on everything.

**Main site shell:** Premium restraint with an edge. Clean but never bland. Typography-forward with technical details (monospace eyebrows, tight spacing, code bracket ghosts as texture). The palette feels elevated, not default SaaS. The shell is the premium container — events are where personality explosions happen.

---

**Hello World event (FORCED LIGHT — pastel cartoon world):**

Theme: **playful, festival energy, cartoon.** This is a celebration of first arrival — bright, warm, welcoming. The dominant feeling is the joy of recognizing a beloved cartoon character, the excitement of a school festival. Think: opening ceremony day, streamers, everyone getting sorted into teams.

- **Palette**: Warm cream base (`#FFFBF4`), sky-to-cream-to-pink hero gradient, per-house pastel fills. Amber (`#F59E0B`) as the throughline accent across all sections.
- **ForceTheme**: `light` — no exceptions. Hello World lives in daylight.
- **Character images**: Official anime/movie artwork used faithfully at full fidelity. Characters should look vivid and true to their source films — do not flatten or desaturate them. `mixBlendMode: multiply` removes white PNG backgrounds on light surfaces cleanly.
- **The 5 houses** (the organizing principle — treat with visual ceremony):
  - 🧽 SpongeBob — yellow pastel (`#FEFCE8`, `#FCD34D`)
  - 🔍 Conan — red pastel (`#FFF1F2`, `#FCA5A5`)
  - 🐼 Kung Fu Panda — green pastel (`#F0FDF4`, `#86EFAC`)
  - 🦊 Zootopia — orange pastel (`#FFF7ED`, `#FDBA74`)
  - 🚀 Toy Story — blue pastel (`#EFF6FF`, `#93C5FD`)
- **Motion**: Float animations, gentle hover bounces, slot-machine text reveals. Festive but not frantic. Always respect `prefers-reduced-motion`.
- **Typography**: Epilogue bold for large display headings, Prompt for Thai text.

**CS101 event (LOCKED IN — do not redesign the theme):**
Super Mario theme — sky blue hero, question blocks, pipes, brick walls, Fredoka font, Mario color palette (#E52521 red, #049CD8 blue, #FBD000 gold, #43B047 green). Iterate within this world; do not replace it.

### Design Principles

1. **Commit to the metaphor.** Every design decision should serve the event's world. On Hello World, every element should feel like part of a cartoon festival. On the shell, every detail should feel like precision tooling. No generic defaults anywhere.

2. **Events are total worlds.** The shell is restrained so events can be total. Hello World is bright daylight cartoon; CS101 is Mario's world. The contrast between event worlds and the dark shell is a feature, not inconsistency.

3. **Faithful to source material.** Character images from official artwork are the hero visual of Hello World. Render them cleanly, scaled to feature rather than decorate. They should look like they came from the actual films, not flattened illustrations.

4. **Dark shell, light events.** Main site shell defaults dark for new visitors (user can toggle). Hello World forces light. CS101 forces its own Mario palette. Theme is per-context, always intentional.

5. **Thai-first typography.** Prompt font must feel as considered as the Latin fonts. Never sacrifice Thai readability for Latin aesthetics. Thai text should look designed, not like an afterthought.

6. **Motion as character.** Animations reveal personality — slot-machine reveals, purposeful floating bubbles, staggered entrance sequences. Motion should make someone say "nice" not "this loaded." Always respect `prefers-reduced-motion`.

7. **Precision over decoration.** Sharp edges, tight spacing, intentional rhythm. Every radius, every shadow, every gap must be chosen — not defaulted. The difference between "technically sharp" and "generic" is intentionality at this level.

### Hard Constraints
- Core routing, registration flows, and MongoDB schemas must not be changed
- CS101 Super Mario theme is locked in — work within it, never replace it
- Hello World houses are **SpongeBob 🧽, Conan 🔍, Kung Fu Panda 🐼, Zootopia 🦊, Toy Story 🚀** — the 5-cartoon house system is the organizing principle (the old card-suit system is gone)
- Hello World pages must force **light** mode (`<ForceTheme theme="light" />`)
- Main site shell defaults dark; user can toggle light/dark
- Thai language support is critical throughout (Prompt font handles Thai glyphs)
- Fonts in use: Geist Sans (`--font-geist-sans`), Geist Mono (`--font-geist-mono`), Epilogue (`--font-epilogue`), Prompt (`--font-prompt`)
- Color system: all values in oklch() — do not introduce hex or hsl tokens at the system level
- Character images use `mixBlendMode: multiply` on light backgrounds to eliminate white PNG borders
